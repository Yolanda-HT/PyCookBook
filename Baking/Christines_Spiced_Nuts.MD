# Christine's Spiced Nuts
> 04/06/2022 <br>
> Servings: 4

## 1. Ingredients

### 1.1 Nuts
- Walnuts: 1/2 cup
- Pecans: 1/2 cup
- Cashews: 1/2 cup
- Almonds: 1/2 cup

### 1.2 Glaze
- Maple syrup: 2 tbsp
- Coconut oil: 1 tbsp
- Rosemary: 1 tsp
- Cumin: 1/2 tsp
- Salt: 1/2 tsp
- Ground black pepper: 1/8 tsp
- Cayenne pepper flakes: 1/8 tsp

## 2. Steps
1. Preheat oven to 350F
2. Stir all ingredients
3. Bake for 15 minutes
