# Yolanda's Mexican Grilled Chicken
> 12/13/2022 <br>
> Servings: 6

## 1. Ingredients
Marinade for 3 lbs boneless skinless chicken
- Minced garlic: 6 cloves
- Lime juice: 2 tbsps
- Olive oil: 4 tbsps
- Chili powder: 3 tbsps
- Cumin: 3 tsps
- Paprika: 3 tsps
- Oregano: 2 tsps
- Black pepper: 1 tsp
- Soy sauce: 1 tbsp
- Adolph's tenderizer: 2 tsps
